About

Our ability to purchase goods online has been revolutionized by mobile devices, which put all the information at our fingertips. More and more customers will turn to other customers for product information instead of the seller's information as information access gets easier. Examples of this kind of information include reviews and ratings left by customers, which have already influenced many customers' purchasing decisions. Customers may make educated decisions and feel confident about them thanks to the transparent system created by the review and ratings platforms offered by eCommerce businesses.

Product reviews may be found in abundance on Amazon.com, and their review system is available through all available channels and presents reviews in an intuitive layout. The product reviewer gives the item a rating between 1 and 5, along with their own opinion based on their whole experience. To get the final product rating, the mean value from all the ratings is determined. By allowing others to vote on whether a review is useful, both the review and the reviewer gain credibility.

Dashboards

A dashboard in Tableau serves as a powerful tool to visually analyze and present your data findings. A dashboard is a consolidated display of multiple interactive visualizations and reports that provide an at-a-glance view of the key metrics and insights derived from your dataset. With Tableau's intuitive drag-and-drop interface, you can easily create dynamic charts, graphs, and maps, allowing you to explore various dimensions of the cell phone reviews data. By leveraging features like filters, drill-down capabilities, and tooltips, you can interactively explore the reviews' sentiments, ratings, and trends across different phone models, brands, or customer segments. Through the use of color-coded indicators, trend lines, and comparative analysis, a well-designed Tableau dashboard can convey valuable insights, such as the most popular phones, top-rated brands, and any emerging patterns or issues in customer reviews. Overall, Tableau's dashboarding capabilities enable you to communicate your data-driven findings effectively and support informed decision-making processes in your Amazon cell phone reviews analysis project.

Story

The story revolves around gaining insights and understanding customer sentiment from a vast collection of cell phone reviews on the Amazon platform. Through data analytics, the project aims to uncover valuable information about customer preferences, satisfaction levels, and common issues related to cell phones available on Amazon. By visualizing the data in Tableau, the story will showcase patterns, trends, and correlations that emerge from the reviews, enabling a comprehensive analysis of customer feedback. The project will provide a deep understanding of customer sentiments, allowing businesses to make data-driven decisions, improve product offerings, and enhance the overall customer experience in the highly competitive cell phone market.
